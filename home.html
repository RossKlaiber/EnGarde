🏆 Project Inspiration

This project was inspired by Ira Pathak, a top-ranked fencer who faced significant challenges navigating the college recruiting process. Our platform aims to eliminate these barriers for international athletes, ensuring equal opportunities to showcase their talent.

🌟 What It Does

  • 🔹 Gives Athletes Exposure 📢 – Players can showcase their skills, stats, and practice tapes to recruiters in a structured, professional format.
  • 🔹 Organizes Recruitment 🎯 – Coaches receive a centralized platform to evaluate, manage, and connect with athletes efficiently.
  • 🔹 Provides Statistical Data 📊 – Advanced analytics & insights help measure a player’s impact on a team, making scouting more data-driven.

🛠️ How We Built It

  • 🔹 Frontend: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap
  • 🔹 Backend: Python, JavaScript
  • 🔹 Frameworks & Libraries: Django, Selenium, BeautifulSoup, Chart.js

🔍 Key Technologies Used:

  • 📌 Django – Manages data and user authentication
  • 📌 Selenium – Automates recruitment processes
  • 📌 BeautifulSoup – Extracts athlete stats from websites
  • 📌 Chart.js – Displays performance trends visually

⚡ Challenges We Faced

  • 🧐 Defining our problem space – Ensuring we solved a real, pressing issue.
  • 💾 Scraping inconsistent data – Navigating the complexities of FIE website structures.
  • ⏳ Managing workloads – Balancing responsibilities within a fast-paced team.

✅ How We Overcame Them:

Strong teamwork, adaptability, and leveraging advanced data-processing tools.

🏅 Accomplishments

  • ✔ Successfully scraped fencing data for athlete profiles.
  • ✔ Applied new libraries to enhance performance tracking.
  • ✔ Built a one-of-a-kind solution—with no direct competitors!

🚀 What’s Next?

  • 🎾 Expanding to other sports – Tennis, wrestling, and more!
  • 📈 Developing advanced stats – Metrics like xW (expected wins) & bWAR (bouts won above replacement).
  • 🌍 Empowering underrepresented athletes – Ensuring global exposure and college opportunities for all.
