Welcome to En Garde

Connecting international fencing athletes with collegiate recruiters.

Project Inspiration

The inspiration for this project comes from Ira Pathak, who, despite being a top-ranked fencer, struggled with the recruiting process. This app aims to bridge that gap for international athletes.

What It Does

  • Gives Athletes Exposure: Players can showcase their skills, stats, and practice tapes to recruiters.
  • Organizes Recruitment: Coaches get a centralized platform to manage and evaluate talent.
  • Provides Statistical Data: Advanced analytics help measure a player's impact on a team.

How We Built It

Frontend: HTML, CSS

Backend: Python, JavaScript

Frameworks: Django, Selenium, BeautifulSoup, Chart.js

Challenges We Faced

Defining our problem space, scraping inconsistent data from the FIE website, and managing workloads were key challenges. Overcoming them emphasized teamwork and adaptability.

Accomplishments

We successfully scraped fencing data, applied new libraries, and built a unique solution that has no direct competitors.

What's Next

  • Expanding to other sports like tennis and wrestling.
  • Developing advanced statistics such as xW (expected wins) and bWAR (bouts won above replacement).
  • Helping underrepresented athletes gain exposure and college opportunities.
